📋 Section 2 — Services Register

We need to make sure the new website has correct and up-to-date information about every Julalikari service. Please review the services below and answer for your service area. Skip services that are not yours.

🏠 Housing Services

Julalikari manages community housing across Tennant Creek and surrounding outstations. Our housing team handles tenancy management, property maintenance, and works to improve housing conditions for Aboriginal families.

  • Tenancy management
  • Property maintenance
  • Housing applications
  • Outstation housing support
Is this information correct?

🛡 Night Patrol

Our Night Patrol service provides community safety by operating evening and night patrols across Tennant Creek. The patrol offers safe transport, assists vulnerable people, and helps reduce harm in the community.

  • Community safety patrols
  • Safe transport home
  • Vulnerable persons support
  • Harm reduction
Is this information correct?

🤝 Community Services

We deliver a range of community programs supporting families, youth, and elders. Our services are designed to strengthen community connections and provide culturally appropriate support.

  • Family support programs
  • Youth activities
  • Elder services
  • Community events
Is this information correct?

🎓 Employment & Training

Our employment and training programs help community members build skills and find meaningful work. We offer job readiness support, vocational training, and pathways to employment.

  • Job readiness programs
  • Vocational training
  • Employment pathways
  • Skills development
Is this information correct?

❤ Health & Wellbeing

Julalikari promotes health and wellbeing through targeted programs addressing chronic disease, mental health, and healthy lifestyles in partnership with health providers.

  • Health promotion
  • Chronic disease programs
  • Mental health support
  • Healthy lifestyle activities
Is this information correct?

🚛 Infrastructure & Municipal

We provide essential infrastructure and municipal services to remote communities and outstations, including road maintenance, waste management, and water supply.

  • Road maintenance
  • Waste management
  • Water supply
  • Essential services

🔧 CDEP / Work Programs
